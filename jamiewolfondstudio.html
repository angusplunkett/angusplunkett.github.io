ANGUS PLUNKETT —> Jamie Wolfond Studio
Project Info
Brand identity for Jamie Wolfond Studio, a product design firm focusing on furniture, lighting and everyday tools. Jamie's work is based around the idea of 'non-newness'; starting with something that exists rather than trying to invent something that doesn't. It was therefore natural for the brand identity to be approched in the same manner. Centered around the website, the identity is designed on one of the world's most heavily used tools for organising information - Google Sheets. This allowed for the creation of every aspect of the new language and subsequent collateral within this simple and accessible framework.
